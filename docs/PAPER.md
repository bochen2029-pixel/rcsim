## Thesis

The kinetic substrate of the Deterrence 2012 mod (MIRV splitting, kinetic anti-ship,
satellite EMP, doomsday device, dead-hand) was built fourteen years before the v5
framework that explains what it was modeling. v5 §8.2's Γ_kinetic term is the
retrospective name for what the 2012 mod already instantiated. The kinetic layer
pre-validates v5 §8.2 structurally because it was built without knowledge of the
framework.
